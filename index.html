🧳 Zaza's College Packing List

🛏️ Room & Sleep

  • Bed sheets (size depends on dorm)
  • Pillow + pillowcase
  • Blanket or comforter
  • Towels (2)
  • Laundry bag or basket
  • Hangers
  • Slippers

🛏️ Clothes & Style

  • Daily outfits (cute & comfy)
  • Pajamas
  • Workout clothes
  • Dressy outfits (1-2)
  • Jacket + warm hoodie
  • Accessories: rings, earrings, scarf
  • Cute shoulder bag + tote bag
  • Slippers + everyday shoes + dress shoes

🛏️ Toiletries & Beauty

  • Shampoo, conditioner, body wash
  • Toothbrush + toothpaste + floss
  • Hairbrush, clips, styling tools (straightener, dryer)
  • Skincare: cleanser, moisturizer, sunscreen
  • Makeup kit + organizer
  • Deodorant + perfume/body mist
  • Menstrual products + pain relief
  • Nail kit, tweezers, cotton pads

🛏️ Study Supplies

  • Backpack or stylish tote
  • Notebooks + pastel pens + highlighters
  • Laptop + charger + USB drive
  • Planner or bullet journal
  • Earbuds or headphones

🛏️ Electronics

  • Phone + charger
  • Power bank
  • Extension cord/power strip
  • USB flash drive

🛏️ Kitchen (if needed)

  • Mug + cup + water bottle
  • Plate, bowl, utensils
  • Pan + small pot
  • Sponge, dish soap, tea towel
  • Snacks, ramen, coffee/tea
  • Mini kettle (if allowed)

🛏️ Documents

  • National ID (CIN)
  • Bac diploma (photocopy)
  • Registration papers
  • Student card (when received)
  • Passport-size photos

🛏️ Extras for Comfort

  • Fairy lights or mini lamp
  • Scented candle or room spray (if allowed)
  • Plushie or comfort item
  • Cozy blanket
  • Personal playlist
  • Hot water bottle for cramps

🛏️ Makeup Brands to Pack or Buy

  • Maybelline (mascara, concealer)
  • Essence (budget-friendly + cute)
  • NYX (lip gloss, eyeliner)
  • L'Oréal (foundation, brow products)
  • e.l.f. (affordable brushes + highlighter)
  • Revolution (eyeshadow palettes)
  • Catrice (powder, blush)

🛏️ Snack & Food Ideas

  • Instant ramen/pasta packs
  • Granola bars
  • Tea bags/instant coffee
  • Chocolate bars/biscuits
  • Dates, nuts, dried fruit
  • Peanut butter or jam
  • Juice boxes or flavored water

🛏️ Outfit Planner Tips

  • Plan 5 go-to outfits for daily wear (rotate!)
  • One "power outfit" for presentations or tough days
  • Keep versatile pieces: jeans, white tee, black top, neutral cardigan
  • Save outfit ideas in your notes or Pinterest board
  • Choose a cohesive color palette to keep packing minimal and cute
  • Keep one statement accessory that elevates every look
